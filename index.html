About Me

I am currently a Phd student at NUS, Singapore, advised by Prof. Lee Gim Hee. Before that, I was a master student in Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University (NJU), advised by Prof. Tong Lu.

My research interests include 3D Computer Vision and Learning-based Geometric Processing. My recent work is about understanding 3D scenes and objects via captured 3D data (such as point clouds and meshes).
